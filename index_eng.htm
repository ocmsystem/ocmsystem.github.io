Advantages and opportunities

  • It is a unique mechanism of the quantitative assessment and integrated management of the organization’s and product’s competitiveness – has no analogues in the world!
  • Allows to determine the place of your organization or products on the market — quantitative assessment of the competitiveness level! And also to compare quantitatively the organization’s and product’s competitiveness of yours and your competitors for all directions; to assess how far competitors keep up/get ahead of you and identify the reasons.
  • Allows to manage the organizational competitiveness:
    • for all activity directions;
    • to identify and eliminate «weak points» of the competitiveness;
    • react timely in the fast-changing market conditions, avoid the crises consequences for competitiveness.
  • It is an invaluable assistant to the Head of the organization in forming the strategy and tactics of increasing the organization’s or product’s competitiveness, keeping competitive advantages; preventing of loss of market share in a crisis or fast-changing market conditions.
