Advantages and opportunities

  • This unique mechanism of the quantitative assessment and integrated management of the organization’s and product’s competitiveness – has no analogues in the world!
  • This proved useful in helping to define your organization’s or products’ place on the market, revealing its quantitative assessment of competitiveness level!
  • It efficiently enables to compare quantitatively your organization’s and product’s competitiveness as well as your competitors’ in all activities; Not only it draws a clear detailed picture of how far competitors go behind /get ahead of you but also identifies the reasons for current situation analysis.
  • It provides an opportunity to manage the organization’s competitiveness in all its ongoing activities, meanwhile
    • Identifying and eliminating «weak points» of the competitiveness;
    • Reacting urgently on the fast-changing market conditions for avoiding the negative consequences for competitiveness.
  • It is an invaluable assistant to the Leading Managers of the organization in the process of forming the strategy and tactics of increasing the organization’s or product’s competitiveness, sustaining competitive advantages; preventing of market share loss in a crisis period as well as fast-changing market conditions.
  • It is an effective boosting tool for improving the mechanisms of state regulation of the competitiveness of industry organizations and the industry as a whole.
